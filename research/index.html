RESEARCH INTERESTS

My principal research interests lie at the intersection of nonprofits studies and fundraising. I am particularly interested in how nonprofits organizations engage with different donors and beneficiaries to raise more donations including use of social media.
My dissertation looks at fundraising strategy of top 30 nonprofit organizations working in the area of international need and understand if representation or portrayal of poor in certain way adds to stereotyping and racial biasness of people of color.

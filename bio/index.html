Rahul Pathak

Academic librarian engaged in front end development, user interface design, usability, and digital publishing workflows for academic publishers committed to open access. Keen to support emerging and non-traditional publishing models and technologies that advance scholarly publishing's transformation. Currently serving as managing editor of the Journal of Electronic Publishing.

Hi, I'm Rahul Pathak. Welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD student in the Andrew Young School at Georgia State University, where I study Public Policy.

My research covers issues related to public finance and social policy, and I have taught courses on policy analysis, program evaluation and applied statistics.

Previously, I worked as a Research Associate at the Centre for Policy Research, New Delhi and the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad. I hold a B.Sc. in Mathematics and Physics from University of Lucknow and M.A. in Development Studies from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai.

Welcome to my personal website.

I am a Doctoral Candidate in Public Administration at the School of Public Administration at University of Nebraska at Omaha. My research focuses on nonprofit studies and public policy. I hold a master's degree in Social Enterpreneurship from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai and bachelors degree in Business Economics from the Aryabhatta College, University of Delhi.

Hi, welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD student in the Andrew Young School at Georgia State University, where I study Public Policy.

My research covers issues related to public finance and social policy and I have taught courses on policy analysis, program evaluation and applied research methods.

Prior to joining the Andrew Young School, I have worked as a Research Associate at the Centre for Policy Research, New Delhi and the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.

I hold Masters degree in Development Studies from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences , Mumbai

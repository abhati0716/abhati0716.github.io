Welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD student in the Andrew Young School at Georgia State University, where I study Public Policy. My research covers issues related to public finance and social policy.

Prior to joining the Andrew Young School, I have worked as Research Associate at the Centre for Policy Research, New Delhi and the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.

I hold Masters degree in Development Studies from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences and an undergraduate degree in Mathematics and Physics from the University of Lucknow.

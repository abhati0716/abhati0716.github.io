Welcome to my personal website. I am a PhD student in the Andrew Young School at Georgia State University, where I study Public Policy.

I also work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Fiscal Research Center. My research focusses on issues related to public finance and social policy. Prior to join the Andrew Young School, I worked as Research Associate at the Centre for Policy Research, New Delhi and the Indian Institute of Management , Ahmedabad.

I hold Masters degree in Development Studies from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai.
